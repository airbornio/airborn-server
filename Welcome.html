Welcome to Airborn!


On Airborn, everything you write is completely private. So feel free to keep your diaries here, or your thoughts about your next invention. We (the makers of Airborn) won't be able to read them even if we wanted to.


Although, if you want to publish your documents, or collaborate on them, you can also do that: click the  menu icon in the top right corner.


From that menu, you can also  print (and export to PDF) or  download as HTML.


You can also  upload ODT files, which you can make with OpenOffice/LibreOffice or export from Microsoft Office.


Feel free to play around with this demo version! Keep in mind that none of your documents are saved in the demo version.


When you're done trying Airborn, head to make an account! (Hold control/command while clicking to open that link.) And after that, sign up for a free 6 month trial of the Knowledge Worker plan to collaborate on documents, keep every old version of your documents, and to get a bit more storage space.


Thanks for trying Airborn! If you have any feedback, feel free to let us know.
